Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Client-Side Processing

ZapPDF is built on the principle of absolute privacy. When you use our service to compress PDF files, all processing happens locally inside your web browser. We use WebAssembly and JavaScript to modify your files without ever transmitting them over the internet.

2. File Storage

We do not have servers to store your files. Your documents never leave your device. Once you close your browser tab, all compressed and original files loaded into memory are permanently deleted from your browser's memory.

3. Data Collection

We do not track, collect, or store any personal data. We do not use third-party analytics services that track your individual usage behavior across the website.

4. Cookies

ZapPDF may use local storage (a browser feature similar to cookies) solely to remember your preferred compression settings (such as chosen compression level) for your convenience. This data never leaves your device.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
