Our Mission

ZapPDF was built with a single goal: to provide a fast, secure, and entirely free way to compress PDF and Image files without compromising privacy. Most tools online require you to upload your sensitive documents to their servers, where they might be stored or analyzed. We do things differently.

By leveraging modern web technologies, ZapPDF performs all compression locally inside your browser. Your files never leave your device.
